Our Features

Prescreened Candidates

We prescreen all GoGENIEs to maintain a high quality candidate pool. Candidates are asked to upload their ID cards and take our psychometric test.

Automated Online Interviews

Play, fast-forward, skip pre-recorded video interviews, which showcase the applicants' communication skills in English Mandarin and Cantonese.

Hire Better

GoGENIE pre-filters applicants and actively pushes candidate to employers using our recommendation algorithm based on job requirement.

Recorded Job History

We take all their job experiences and skills set, and compile for them a standardize, simplified profile that resides on our platform.

Reliable Rating System

Employers and GoGENIEs can review each other after each job. We are building a community that encourages fairness and communication.

Super Fast

Your post will reach all our GoGENIE's mobile phones instantly and you can call applicants directly with your mobile phone. It is so convenient and fast.
