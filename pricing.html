Frequently Asked Questions

How does pricing work & what’s included in the fee?

Our monthly pricing is simple and transparent at HK$200 per month plus HK$30 per employee. This basic package covers 1 active job post and our shift management system. We guarantee there is no hidden charges. You are only charged for the number of employees processed that month according to your shift management, regardless of the total number of people you have in your account.

Is there an additional cost if I have more than 1 store to manage?

Fees will be charged as per legal entity per headcount. There is no additional fee for setting up multiple stores within your account.

How do I get started?

It’s easy! Sign up for our 60-day FREE TRIAL here.

How does the 60-day free trial work?

No payment information is needed to sign up for GoGENIE. Your first 60 days are free and you can manage unlimited number of employees, plus 1 active job posting. Once your first 60 days have passed, pricing starts at HK$200 per month plus HK$30 per person.

Do I have to sign a long-term contract?

We are flexible in our offering. With monthly subscription, there are no obligations, pay as you go. Love us so much? Why not buy an annual package now and you can save more for your business.

What if I need to add more employees?

We will automatically update your monthly price based on the number of employee(s) you manage that month. For example, if you have 10 employees on your company’s account but you only 6 employees worked, you will only be charged for 6 employees that month.

How do I pay after my free trial?

After your free trial is up, you will be prompted with our subscription plan. You can pay online with your credit cards via our online payment gateway (we use Strip and its secured!). Alternatively, if you'd like to pay via cheque or internet banking, let us know and you will receive an invoice from us.
